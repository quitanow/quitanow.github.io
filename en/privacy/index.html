Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Thank you for using Gambling Recovery Guide. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to handling any data related to this app clearly and carefully.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Gambling Recovery Guide app and the related pages on quitanow.com.

2. Data we may handle

Depending on how you use the app, this may include:

  • Daily check-in records
  • Urge logs, trigger scenes, and self-reminder notes
  • Support phrases, favourites, and personal notes
  • Relapse reset records and recovery plans

3. How data is stored

The current version stores all data locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to external servers. If we ever add account sync, cloud backup, or analytics, we will update this policy first and clearly explain what changes.

4. How we use data

Data is used only to provide and improve the following features:

  • Saving your progress and personalised content
  • Letting you review past records and support phrases
  • Helping you enter the self-help flow faster in the moment
  • Improving app stability and usability

5. Third-party services

The current version does not use third-party advertising, tracking, or cloud analytics services. If we add any in the future, we will clearly describe them in this policy.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share the self-help content you enter in the app with any third party, except where required by law or necessary to protect user safety.

7. Your choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • If the app provides a reset or clear function, you can delete local data directly in the app.
  • For further help, contact us via the support page or email below.

8. Children

This app is not designed for children. If you are a minor, we recommend using it with a parent or guardian's knowledge.

9. Policy updates

When features or data handling change, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, visit our support page or email us at: support@quitanow.com
